.NET Aspire provides features for running and connecting multi-project applications and their dependencies for local development
environments.

Components: .NET Aspire components are NuGet packages for commonly used services, such as Redis or Postgres, with standardized
interfaces ensuring they connect consistently and seamlessly with your app.

Tooling: .NET Aspire comes with project templates and tooling experiences for Visual Studio and the dotnet CLI help you create 
and interact with .NET Aspire apps.
